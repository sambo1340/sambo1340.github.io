Drinking beer is easier than you think. All you need to know is what kind of beer to try first. Here is a short list of popular types of beer.

LAGER

Lagers are known for their crisp, clean taste. This is because of the fermentation process, which involves yeasts that settle on the bottom of the fermenter. Common styles of lager are: pale lager and Dunkel.

ALE

What do ales and Betty White's social security number have in common? They both date back to ancient times. Ales are brewed all over the world and tend to be sweet, full-bodied beers. Common styles of ales are: IPA and pale ale.

STOUT

Stouts are unique in that they use unmalted, dark-roasted barley, low amount of hops and often sweet, caramel or chocolate aromas. The most famous stout is Guinness. Common styles of stout are: cream and caramel stouts.

PORTER

A porter is a very dark ale, made from heavy roasted malt. Porters can taste sweet or very hoppy, since the amount of alt and hops is variable.

WHEAT BEER

Wheat beers often have many different names, one of the most common being hefeweizen These beers are refreshing, pale in color and highly carbonated.
